ERP and Mobile App

Complete College Management with
Powerful ERP Modules & Smart Mobile App

Our ERP solution is divided into 4 major categories, ensuring complete digital control over college operations:

  • Core Modules – Admission, Fees, Accounts, Exams, Timetable, and Library Management
  • Resource Management – HR & Payroll, Inventory, Hostel, and Transport Management.
  • Communication Management – Bulk SMS, Email, Parent-Teacher Connect, and Notifications.
  • Data & System Integration – Biometric Attendance, Payment Gateway, and Advanced Analytics Dashboard.

The eAcademics mobile application ensures a real-time digital bridge between college, parents, and students:

  • Track daily activities, assignments, and academic progress
  • Receive instant updates via SMS, email, and push notifications.
  • Manage fees and other payments directly from the app.
